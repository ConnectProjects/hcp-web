TechTool - Full Field Manual

Connect Hearing Industrial Division - Field Testing Guide

1. What is TechTool?

TechTool is the field component of the HCP-Web platform. It runs in a browser on your phone, tablet, or laptop and works fully offline. No internet is required on-site.

TechTool stores packets locally using IndexedDB. Data persists but is tied to this specific browser. Submit packets as soon as you have a connection.

2. Getting Started

Setting up your profile

  1. Enter your full name and initials. These are recorded on every test.
  2. Enter your folder name — the subfolder in OneDrive for your packets.
  3. Enter your IAT number (certification number).

Connecting the sync folder

Tap ↓ Sync. Navigate to the root of your OneDrive folder. TechTool looks in techs/[initials] automatically.

3. Dashboard

Shows today's assignments and sync status. Cards turn green with a ✓ Done label when all employees are tested/skipped.

4. Company Screen

Displays site details and Sticky Notes from the office. Read these before starting testing.

5. Employee List

Shows every employee in the packet. Indicators show who is:

  • ✓ Tested (Complete)
  • Pending (Waiting)
  • Skipped (Not tested today)

6. Test Entry

Record thresholds from your audiometer. Frequencies from 0.5k to 8k Hz. NR (No Response) is supported for high-threshold readings.

7. Classification

Categories are auto-calculated. You cannot override these on-site.

ColorMeaning
GreenNormal Results
YellowEarly Warning / STS
RedAbnormal / Referral Required

8. Counsel & Referrals

Edit the pre-populated counselling text as needed. For Abnormal or STS results, you **must** complete a paper referral form on-site. Use the "Print Referral Form" button to generate a detailed form if you have a mobile printer.

9. Full Visit Workflow

Before the visit

  1. Open TechTool while online.
  2. Tap ↓ Sync to download packets.
  3. Prepare paper referral forms.

On-Site

  1. Record thresholds into TechTool.
  2. Review Classification and edit Counsel.
  3. Finalize and Save each test.

Ending the visit

When done, tap **Submit Packet →**. This drops the result JSON into the OneDrive inbox subfolder.

10. Troubleshooting

Sync finds nothing: confirm your folder initials in Settings matches the folder on OneDrive.

Submit fails: Grant browser permission to the folder again by tapping "Reconnect".
